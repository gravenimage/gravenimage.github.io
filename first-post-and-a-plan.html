First post and a plan.

Locked. Down.

With 2 young children, there's not much change. But after 7 (???) weeks, it is beginning to feel a little constricting. And working from home, I'm finding it more important than ever to get more organised, in work and in home life.

So, as a little experiment, I'm moving a lot of my life into org-mode, and this is the outcome. A blog might seem old-fashioned, but it feels a little less self-indulgent than a snarky twitter feed or a look-at-me Facebook post. And as for Instagram etc - kids these days.

Harumph.

So, I'll try and blog something every 2 days. It might be nothing, it might be about programming, it might be about life in Norway (which I'm slowly acclimatizing to). We'll see. No promises.
