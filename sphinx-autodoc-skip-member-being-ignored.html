Sphinx autodoc-skip-member being ignored

Published: Mon 20 July 2015
By gravenimage

In misc.

Argh, I've just struggled for an hour to figure out why the results of my autodoc-skip-member handler in Sphinx are consistently being ignored....

It's because

a) you can only have one handler (app.connect does not chain handlers), and

b) I am using the napoleon extension, which installs its own autodoc-skip-member handler.

In my particular case, I needed to skip all special members (e.g. __dict__, __str__ etc). Luckily, napoleon allows you to add this to your conf.py:

napoleon_include_special_with_doc = False
